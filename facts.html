Facts about Wong Tai Sin

Below is some of the major statistics about Wong Tai Sin (WTS) based on 2021 Population Census, conducted by the Census and Statistics Department of the Hong Kong government.

The statistics below can demonstrate the following characteristics of Wong Tai Sin:

  • High population density
  • Old population
  • Low socio-economic status
