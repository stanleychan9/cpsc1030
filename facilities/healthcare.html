Healthcare in Wong Tai Sin

What to do if you are sick in Hong Kong? The healthcare services in Hong Kong is predominately provided by the public sector - it is much cheaper to visit public clinics and hospitals than the private ones. However, for the middle-class or above who usually have purchased private medical insurance, they may go to private clinics and hospitals for quicker and better services.

The public clinics and hospitals are generally managed by the Hospital Authority (HA), a statutory body established in 1990. While board members of the HA are appointed by the Hong Kong government, it is not part of the government structure.

The HA divides its services into eight clusters based on geographical locations. Wong Tai Sin is part of the Kowloon Central Cluster, which serves not only Wong Tai Sin, but also two other districts, namely Yau Tsim Mong and Kowloon City.

Therefore, when necessary, residents of Wong Tai Sin may be sent to a hospital in a nearby district. For example, when my mother went to hospital for her special treatment a few years ago, she went to Queen Elizabeth Hospital that is located in Yau Tsim Mong. Certainly, when having mild illness such as a common cold, it is more convenient for a patient to go to a public clinic within Wong Tai Sin!
