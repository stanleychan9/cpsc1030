Welcome to Wong Tai Sin!

Hong Kong is divided into 18 administrative districts, located in the three regions of Hong Kong i.e. Hong Kong Island, Kowloon Peninsula, and the New Territories.

Wong Tai Sin is one of the districts situated in Kowloon Peninsula, alongside Kwun Tong, Kowloon City and Sham Shui Po.

To know how to pronounce "Wong Tai Sin" in Cantonese, the major language used in Hong Kong, please see the audio file below.

While Hong Kong has a long coastal line near the Pacific Ocean, Wong Tai Sin is landlock - the only district in Hong Kong with this characteristic.

Photo credit: Wikimedia Commons, Wylve

There is no official division of Wong Tai Sin into different areas. Based on my experience, the district can roughly be divided into:

  • Wong Tai Sin, Chuk Yuen, Tsz Wan Shan and San Po Kong
  • Lok Fu and Wang Tau Hom
  • Diamond Hill and Hammer Hill
  • Ngau Chi Wan and Ping Shek

This website introduces the basics of this old urban district - statistics, post-WWII history, and facilities. Don't forget to play in the quiz to test your knowledge about this district!
